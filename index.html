Privacy policy

Applies to mobile applications published by wegrewup, including NFT.

Summary

  • NFT is a local file manager. Your files stay on your device unless you connect cloud or remote storage.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Cloud sign-in uses official Google, Microsoft, and Dropbox authentication — we never receive your cloud passwords.

Information we access

Depending on features you use, the app may access:

  • Files and media — browse, organize, preview, copy, move, and delete items you select.
  • Storage statistics — usage breakdowns and cleanup suggestions.
  • Network — remote hosts, cloud providers, LAN sharing, and updates.
  • Biometrics / credentials — only if you enable app lock.
  • Purchase information — optional support membership via Google Play Billing.

Stored on your device

  • App settings, favorites, recent files, and browse history
  • Encrypted vault keys (if vault is enabled)
  • Remote host credentials you save locally
  • Recycle bin metadata

Cloud and remote services

Google Drive, OneDrive, and Dropbox authentication is handled by each provider. The app receives tokens to list and download files you open. We do not operate backend servers for your file content.

LAN sharing

Wi‑Fi sharing runs a temporary server on your local network. Only people with your link or QR on the same network can access the chosen folder. Stop sharing anytime.

Third-party services

  • Google Play Billing — optional membership
  • Buy Me a Coffee — optional tips in browser
  • Google / Microsoft / Dropbox — optional cloud sign-in

Data we do not collect

NFT does not require a wegrewup account. We do not upload your files to wegrewup servers or track file names for advertising.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13.
