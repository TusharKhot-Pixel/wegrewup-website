Privacy policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This privacy policy applies to mobile applications published by wegrewup, including NFT (“the app”). We respect your privacy and design our apps to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

Summary

  • NFT is a local file manager. Your files stay on your device unless you choose to connect cloud or remote storage.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Cloud sign-in uses official Google, Microsoft, and Dropbox authentication — we do not receive your cloud passwords.

Information we access

Depending on features you use, the app may access:

  • Files and media on your device — to browse, organize, preview, copy, move, and delete items you select.
  • Storage statistics — to show usage breakdowns and cleanup suggestions.
  • Network — for remote hosts (FTP/SFTP/SMB), cloud providers, LAN sharing, and app updates.
  • Biometrics / device credentials — only if you enable app lock.
  • Purchase information — if you buy optional support membership through Google Play Billing.

Information stored on your device

  • App settings and preferences
  • Favorites, recent files, and browse history
  • Encrypted vault keys (if you use the vault feature)
  • Remote host credentials you save (stored locally on your device)
  • Recycle bin metadata

Cloud and remote services

If you connect Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox, authentication is handled by the respective provider. The app receives access tokens to list and download files you open. We do not operate those services and do not store your cloud passwords on our servers — we have no backend servers for NFT file content.

LAN sharing

When you start Wi‑Fi sharing, the app runs a temporary file server on your local network. Only people with the link or QR code on the same network can access the folder you choose. Stop sharing at any time to end the server.

Third-party services

  • Google Play Billing — optional membership purchases
  • Buy Me a Coffee — optional tip links opened in your browser
  • Google / Microsoft / Dropbox — optional cloud sign-in

Data we do not collect

NFT does not require a wegrewup account. We do not upload your files to wegrewup servers. We do not track your file names or contents for advertising.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
